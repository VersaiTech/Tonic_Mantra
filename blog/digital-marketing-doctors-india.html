Digital Marketing for Doctors in India: 9 Insane Ways to Reach Your Patients Online

Mar 02, 2023

Healthcare Marketing Strategy & TIps

Imagine a patient, who recently moved to your location where your practice is located. He has been suffering from chronic back pain for some time. Since he doesn’t know anyone he turns to Google to find one. He simply typed in "chronic pain specialist near me," and your practice pops up at the top of the search results. 

They click on your website and are impressed by the information that you’ve written about chronic pain management on your website

Later that day, the patient comes in for an appointment, and during the consultation, they mention that they chose your clinic because they found you online and appreciated the educational resources you provided. They feel confident that you have the expertise to treat their condition and decide to become a regular patient.

Without digital marketing, it's possible that this patient may have never found your practice, or they may have chosen a different provider. 

So, in the rest of the article. I’ll share with you the details of digital marketing strategies for doctors that can be used to make sure your clinic pops up when searched online.

So let’s dive in…

Some other ways in which online marketing for doctors can benefit your practice include:

  • The abundance of online channels and social media platforms in india allows you to target audiences best fitted to your practice

  • You can easily interact and engage with your patients on an ongoing basis, addressing grievances and ensuring prompt redressal before their negative reviews affect your practice's reputation

  • You can also establish yourself as a thought leader by regularly posting relevant content to your website or social media handles to increase patient awareness about your practice

Easy to Implement Digital Marketing Tips for Doctors in India to attract patients

Don't treat digital marketing as an expense but rather an investment.

Digital marketing in healthcare is a very complicated affair involving a high level of planning and strategic approach. (Which you will see below) So don't think of it as an unnecessary expense in your pocket. For every penny you invest in it, you can get ten fold a return.

1. Identify the Right Keywords

The success of any website largely depends on the keywords that they are targeting. It helps search engines understand your content and provide it to the user whenever they search online. 

So, your first step would be to identify the kind of keywords that your patients use when searching for your services. 

Suppose you are an eye care hospital operating in Mumbai And want to attract local patients. The right keywords can be:

  • Eye care specialist near me
  • Eye clinic near me
  • Doctors who treat eye near me
  • Eye care tips for elderly patients

Etc… etc.

After, you have finished finalizing your keywords. It’s time to frame your content.

Don’t know what keywords are right for your website? You could hire a freelance digital marketing consultant or hire a full time Healthcare digital marketing agency.

2. Take Your Practice Online

In the present digital era, your medical website is often the first impression your patients will have of you. It is critical to have a user-friendly website with clear messaging that helps your patients navigate your services easily. It will save patients time and effort, simplify their search and selection process and provide valuable features like booking an appointment, downloading reports, 24/7 support through chatbots, etc.

Your twitter, Linkedin, facebook account can be banned but nobody can take down your website. In other words, you should treat your website as a digital asset.

2. Provide Patients With High Value Content Through Your Blog

When was the last time you passed away a free opportunity to grab something like discounted food, discounted cloth or even offers like Buy 1 get 1 free. I guess never.

That’s how your patients behave. They crave free tips or information that others might charge them for.

But Ajay, why would I give free tips and information when others are charging for it?

That’s simple - When patients are given something for free, they tend to value it even more. This is known as the “reciprocity principle" in psychology. 

By offering free value, you will build trust with your patients. It shows that you are knowledgeable and generous, which can lead to increased loyalty and engagement.

Free valuable content can also help you in establishing your personal brand as a thought leader in your industry. It can also increase your visibility and reach, as people are more likely to share and recommend content that they find helpful.

Let me show you an example.Below is the screenshot of Google analytics data of traffic and conversion of one of our US based healthcare client. What you see here is trust and loyalty in the form of data.

They provide free valuable tips to their patients and in return get so much trust from them

Blogging is effective tool when it comes to brand positioning for doctors and physicians. By writing and posting blog content on a regular basis, you can bridge the gap between you and your patients while providing meaningful information. Make sure to write on topics that are related to your practice.

Pro TIP: You can share a recent update reagrding your practice or new insights into the latest treatment options or just couple of home remedies to educate your patients while encouraging them to share your post for wider reach. 

3. Use WhatsApp to Engage Patients

As the most popular global mobile messenger app, WhatsApp can play a crucial role in helping you engage with your patients on a more personal level, right when they need it the most. From appointment reminders to 24/7 patient support, you can employ WhatsApp chatbots to reach out to more patients simultaneously. 

The ease of use offered by WhatsApp is the foremost reason why many healthcare practices and hospitals use the AI-powered platform.

  • To schedule appointments for consultations and checkup
  • To send test results and reports, saving the patients from physically collecting them
  • To deliver daily health-related tips and instant notifications for exercising, maintaining a balanced diet, etc.

4. Be Active on Social Media

Never underestimate the power of social media in creating a good impression on prospective patients and getting more online recommendations. Social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter make it easier for doctors to share updates with their patients and stay connected anywhere, at any time. Here are a few social media marketing strategies you may want to implement for your practice-

  • Conduct free webinars or answer health-related queries in real-time

  • Respond to patient grievances through Facebook's online groups and discussion boards

  • Target the right audience on different social media channels based on their distinct demographics and interests

  • Employ social media marketing experts who can help promote your practice on different social media channels

5. Tell Compelling Patient Stories

Sharing patient stories and testimonials will not only motivate others experiencing similar health issues but will also establish you as a credible healthcare practice that is genuinely invested in its patient's wellbeing. You can even have a dedicated web page displaying patient stories as shared by them, their family, or your staff. 

As you can see from the image below that how one of the leading hospitals using their patient stories to reach thousands of more users and engage them on personal level.

The idea is to connect your practice and prospective patients seeking doctors who can treat them with a human touch. By sharing patient experiences that clearly elucidate how you went the extra mile to provide personalized care for their unique needs, you can effortlessly attract new patients to your clinic.

6. Invest in Paid Search Advertising

Perhaps the easiest way to gain instant visibility, carefully planned and well-executed paid ad campaigns can help your practice stand out among a sea of competitors. What more, you can target the best fit for your practice by ensuring that your ads are visible to the right audience at the right time.

Below you can see an example of paid ads campaign for a gynecology practice in Hyderabad for the highlighted search term.

Platforms like Facebook and LinkedIn allow you to run paid ad campaigns centered around terminologies and search terms that new prospects might use while searching for your practice. You can even adjust these campaigns to suit your budget, thus saving thousands from being spent on futile marketing.

7. Get Listed on Local Listing Directories

Your practice must be listed on local directories like Justdial, Sulekha, etc., and on medical directories like Practo, HealthGrades, DoctorsDig.com, and BookofDoctors to be easily found by prospective patients. However, it is not just enough to be listed. You need to provide complete and accurate information a  bout your medical practice, no matter which online directory you use.

Accurate directory listings will also ensure greater visibility across the web as Google's bots search through every bit of data related to your practice available online to establish consistency and credibility.

8. Create Google Business Profile (If you haven't already)

Perhaps the best way to attract local patients to your clinic, even more than your website

How? 

Whenever you search for doctors or businesses near you, Google displays a list of businesses in a box at the top of the page. This is called  “Google Map Pack” (or the Google Local Map Pack) is a prominent section in the Google local search results that showcases the top-ranking local listings for your location or the search location.

This is how it looks from patient’s perspective

This is an important place for doctors to show up as the top 3 spots get the highest clicks to their profile.

So, go create one if you haven’t already.

Don’t know how, read this step by step guide on how to create a Google business profile.

A lot of people ask questions on quora even for their health> That makes it the Goldmine of a platform. All you have to do is create your account and start identifying questions related to your field and answer them with insights.
